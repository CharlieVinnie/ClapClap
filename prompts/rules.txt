I have a game called "ClapClap". The rules are as follows:

1. The game is played between two players.

2. The game is round-based, with two players playing a hand simultaneously in each round.

3. After each round, whether one of the player wins or the game continues is based only on the comparison of the hands played by the players.

4. The game ends when one of the player wins the game.

5. All the possible hands are: ”气“，“盾”，“gi”，“破”，“冷风”，“如来”，“黑洞”，“小火”，“闪电”，“大火”，“Shining”，“闪”，“吃”，“双吃”，“十字防”。

6. Each player has 5 types resources: qi, shield, spark, battery, and duck. Initially duck=2 and others are all 0.

7. 气 盾 can be played at all times without resource comsumption. 气 adds 1 qi, 盾 adds one shield.

8. gi requires 1 qi, 破 requires 2 qi, 冷风 requires 3 qi, 如来 requires 5 qi, 黑洞 requires 8 qi。

9. 小火 requires 2 shield, 闪电 requires 3 shield, 大火 requires 4 shield, Shining requires 6 shield.

10. 闪 requires 1 duck, 吃 requires 1 qi, 双吃 requires 2 qi, 十字防 requires 2 qi。

10. The attack hands are ordered as follows: 黑洞 > Shining = 如来 > 大火 = 冷风 > 闪电 = 破 > 小火 > gi。 

11. When a stronger attack hand meets a weaker attack hand, the stronger one wins. If the strength of two attack hands are equal, the game continues.

12. All attack hands can kill 气。Only those stronger or equal to 破 can kill 盾。Those stronger or equal to 如来 can kill 十字防。

13. “闪” ducks all attacks unconditionally.

14. “吃” kills 破, "eats" 闪电，but is killed by all other attack hands.

15. “双吃” kills 破, "eats" 闪电 and Shining。

16. Each 小火 gathers 1 spark, Each 闪电 gathers 1 battery. 2 sparks can be used to play 大火 (instead of 4 shield), 2 batteries can be used to play Shining (instead of 6 shield).

17. An exception to 16 is that if a 闪电 is "eaten" by a 吃，then no batteries are gathered.


Some caveats:

“吃” "eats" 闪电 with the game continuing.

盾 is different from 十字防。冷风 kills 盾 because 冷风 > 破, which is the minimal undefensable hand for 盾。

吃 does NOT defeat 闪电, the game continues instead. Same for 双吃 and 闪电、 双吃 with Shining。 

Use sparks primarily instead of shields when playing 大火。Same for Shining.

All attacks besides 破 and 闪电 and Shining kills 双吃。
